# Foundation Concept

Status: 2026-05-27

Working title: Dreher Foundation for Global Health.

This is a concept, not a registered foundation structure.

## Purpose

If NALA-DNA-Med or related tooling helps generate commercial value, a defined share of that value should ideally support:

- open biomedical research
- student and school access
- AI workspaces and compute credits
- low-power device research programs
- public datasets and public wiki maintenance
- grants for neglected or specific disease research
- responsible clinical collaboration infrastructure

## Governance Idea

- independent foundation board or advisory council
- transparent grant criteria
- annual public report
- conflict-of-interest rules
- scientific and medical advisory review
- student/researcher access programs

## Relationship To Open Source

Open source access and commercial revenue-sharing are separate legal questions. The project can be open source while also asking commercial users to contribute back through foundation agreements, certification, support contracts, dual licensing, donations, or partner terms.

The exact mechanism needs legal review.
