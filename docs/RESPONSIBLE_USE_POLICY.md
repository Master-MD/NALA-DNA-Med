# Responsible Use Policy Draft

Status: 2026-05-27

NALA-DNA-Med is intended for public-interest biomedical research support, education, source organization, and hypothesis generation. It must not be used for harmful, illegal, or deceptive purposes.

## Allowed Direction

- literature review assistance
- source organization
- research hypothesis tracking
- non-clinical demos
- student learning
- local AI workflow experimentation
- safety-focused comparison of mechanisms, risks, and evidence

## Prohibited Direction

- diagnosis or treatment without qualified medical responsibility
- prescription or dosing advice
- emergency medical decisions
- biological, chemical, or physical harm
- weaponization or harmful optimization of biological agents
- evading safety controls
- uploading third-party clinical data without consent and legal basis
- impersonating doctors, researchers, regulators, or patients
- using the app to mislead patients or sell unsupported cures

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the data they upload, the laws that apply to them, and any downstream use of generated outputs. The project owner and contributors do not endorse illegal, harmful, abusive, or clinically unsafe use.

## Project Boundary

The software can help organize questions. It cannot validate a therapy. Any real therapeutic direction requires independent scientific review, laboratory validation, clinical trials, regulatory approval where required, and qualified medical responsibility.
